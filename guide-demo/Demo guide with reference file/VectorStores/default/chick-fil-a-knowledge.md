# Chick-fil-A Athens — Restaurant Reference Guide

This document is the reference knowledge base for the Chick-fil-A restaurant at 1234 Road Pkwy, Athens, GA. It covers the full menu with prices, breakfast, treats, sauces, allergen information, nutrition, catering, ordering options, the rewards app, gift cards, policies, holiday hours, and hiring.

## Location, Contact, and Hours

Chick-fil-A Athens is located at 1234 Road Pkwy, Athens, GA, at the corner of Road Parkway and Milledge Avenue, in the shopping center next to the grocery store. Parking is free in the shared lot.

Hours: Monday through Saturday, 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Closed every Sunday. The dining room closes at 9:00 PM; the drive-thru stays open until the 10:00 PM close. Breakfast is served from opening until 10:30 AM, when the lunch menu begins.

## Ordering Options

We do not take orders over the phone. Guests can order:

- At the front counter inside the restaurant.
- In the drive-thru, which has two lanes and team members taking orders face-to-face during peak hours.
- On the Chick-fil-A One mobile app, for pickup at the counter, drive-thru express lane, or curbside. Mobile orders skip the line.
- Curbside: order in the app, park in a numbered curbside spot, and a team member brings the food out.
- Delivery: available through DoorDash, Uber Eats, and Grubhub, and through Chick-fil-A Delivery in the app for addresses within about a 10 minute drive of the restaurant. Third-party delivery prices are slightly higher than in-store prices.

## Chick-fil-A One Rewards App

The Chick-fil-A One app is free on the App Store and Google Play. Guests earn points on every purchase: 10 points per dollar at the Member level, 11 points per dollar at Silver, 12 at Red, and 13 at Signature. Points can be redeemed in the app for free menu items, starting around 350 points for a hash brown or small drink and up to about 4,000 points for a 30-count nugget tray. The app also gets occasional free reward offers and is the only way to order curbside pickup.

## Allergen Information

All breaded chicken is pressure-cooked in fully refined peanut oil. Refined peanut oil has the allergenic proteins removed and is generally not considered an allergen by the FDA, but guests with peanut allergies should talk with a team member in the restaurant before ordering.

- Sandwich buns contain wheat, milk, soy, and egg.
- Breaded chicken (sandwiches, nuggets, strips) contains wheat, milk, and egg.
- Grilled chicken filets and grilled nuggets contain milk and soy, but no wheat — grilled nuggets and grilled filets with no bun are the most common gluten-sensitive choices.
- A certified gluten-free bun is available for $1.50 extra. It arrives individually wrapped, and guests assemble the sandwich themselves to limit cross-contact.
- Waffle fries are cooked in canola oil, separately from the chicken, and contain no wheat, egg, or dairy ingredients.
- Milkshakes, Icedream, frosted lemonade, and mac and cheese contain milk. The chocolate chunk cookie contains wheat, egg, milk, and soy.
- The kale crunch side contains almonds. The market salad contains tree nuts in the granola topping.

All items are prepared in shared kitchens, so cross-contact with peanuts, tree nuts, wheat, milk, egg, and soy is possible with any item. For any specific allergy concern, guests should speak with a manager in the restaurant before ordering. Team members cannot guarantee any item is allergen-free.

## Store Policies

- Refunds: if an order is wrong or unsatisfactory, bring the receipt or app order number to the counter, or use the "Report a Problem" option in the app, and the restaurant will remake the item or refund it.
- Lost and found: items are kept at the counter for 30 days. Call or ask a team member.
- Wi-Fi: free guest Wi-Fi in the dining room, no password required.
- Play area: an indoor play area for children 54 inches and under, open during dining room hours. Socks required.
- Restrooms are for customers and are inside the dining room.
- Pets: only service animals are allowed inside. Leashed pets are welcome in the drive-thru.
- Large groups are welcome in the dining room, but we don't take reservations or have a private party room.
- We don't have a standing military, student, or first-responder discount, but promotional offers appear in the Chick-fil-A One app.

## Community, Fundraising, and Donations

The restaurant hosts Spirit Night fundraisers for local schools, teams, and nonprofits: the organization picks an evening, invites supporters to dine, and receives a percentage of that night's sales. Requests should be made at least three weeks ahead by asking for the marketing director at the counter or through the restaurant's local Facebook page. Donation requests for raffle baskets or gift cards are reviewed monthly and should also be submitted at least three weeks in advance.

## Employment

The restaurant hires team members ages 15 and up for front counter, drive-thru, and kitchen positions, both part-time and full-time, with flexible scheduling around school. Apply on the Chick-fil-A careers website by searching for the Athens location, or ask for an application at the counter. Team members are eligible for scholarship programs, free meals during shifts, and Sundays off every week. For hiring questions, ask to speak with a manager.

## Holiday and Special Hours

The restaurant is closed every Sunday year-round. It is also closed on Thanksgiving Day, Christmas Day, and July 4th. On Christmas Eve the restaurant closes early at 6:00 PM, and on New Year's Eve it closes at 8:00 PM. New Year's Day opens late at 8:00 AM. Any other special hours are posted on the door and on the restaurant's local Facebook page.

## Frequently Asked Questions

- Why are you closed on Sunday? Our founder Truett Cathy decided in 1946 that the restaurant would close on Sundays so employees could have a day for rest, family, or worship, and every Chick-fil-A has kept that practice since.
- Do you take phone orders? No — orders can be placed at the counter, in the drive-thru, or on the Chick-fil-A One app.
- Is the peach milkshake available? Only seasonally, typically June through August.
- Do you serve breakfast all day? No, breakfast ends at 10:30 AM.
- Can I book the play area or a party? No reservations or private parties, but large groups are welcome anytime.
- Do you deliver? Yes, through the Chick-fil-A app, DoorDash, Uber Eats, and Grubhub.
